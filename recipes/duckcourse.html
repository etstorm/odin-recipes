Duck Course

Description:

This course, was created as part of a multi-course tasting menu. The dish features seared duck breast, a grilled swiss chard salad, and a wonderful yam puree.

Ingredients:

  • Duck Breast
  • Swiss Chard
  • Yams
  • Fish Sauce
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Pomegranate juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps:

  1. Peel and dice yams
  2. Toss into boiling water and cook until easily pierced with a fork, remove
  3. Puree in a food processor with heavy whipping cream, and season with salt and pepper. Set aside
  4. Heat a pan over medium high heat
  5. Begin preparing the duck breast by scoring the skin in a hatch pattern
  6. Season liberally with salt and pepper
  7. Once pan is up to temp, place the duck skin side down (do not touch the duck for a few minutes, in order to let the skin form a crust)
  8. Begin to heat up the grill pan, or a full size grill
  9. Place leaves of swiss chard on the grill, until a char begins to appear, remove
  10. Slice the leaves length wise to remove the stems
  11. Then slice into bit sized pieces
  12. Flip the duck and let sear on the other side until internal temp registers 145ยบ
  13. In a separate pan reduce pomegranate juice, along with fish sauce, until thickened
  14. Remove duck from the pan, and slice into strips against the grain of the meat
  15. Begin to plate, placing a spoon full of puree on the plate, along with swiss chard salad, and a few slices of duck, spoon sauce over the duck
